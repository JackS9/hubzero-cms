Courses

Overview

Items found here are essentially catalog entries. This includes the name of the course

Managers

Managers of a course have full permissions to create, edit, or delete content for any offering or section within the course.

Offerings

An offering contains all the course materials such as videos, slides, homeworks, and exams.

When to create a new offering

A new offering should only be created when significant changes have been made to the course materials. A typo (misspelling or error in grammar) would not be considered significant.

Pages

This is supplementary information related to the course as a whole and not specific to particular offering. The most common page will be "Frequently Asked Questions" (FAQs) about the course containing information on "How is the course going to work?" or "Why should I take these courses?". Information concerning specific lecture content is discouraged as lecture content is subject to change between offerings.
